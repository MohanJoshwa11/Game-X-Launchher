FAQ


Fall Guys offers Virtual Currency in the form of Show-Bucks for sale on both the Epic Game Store and its in-game store. Fall Guys also offers various bundles
that include cosmetic in-game products and Show-Bucks.
Using Show-Bucks that are earned (through the Season Pass) or purchased, players can also purchase cosmetic items in the in-game Item Shop,
along with purchasing the Season Pass.

Example in-app purchases include the following Show-Bucks offerings:
1,000 Show-Bucks
2,800 Show-Bucks
5,000 Show-Bucks
13,500 Show-Bucks
Example premium Season Pass rewards:
30 Costumes, including Xeonomorph, Spock, and Hatsune Miku
1 Pattern
1 Colour
1,100 Show-Bucks
3 Faceplates
3 Nameplates
4 Emotes
3 Celebrations
Does the product use Epic Games' payment platform or your own for these in-app purchases? Note: Epic can not provide details or refund for
any transactions made outside of Epic's payment platform.
When playing Fall Guys through the Epic Game Store, all in-app purchases use Epic Games’ payment platform. The in-game store transitions the user
to the platform storefront to complete purchases. When playing on other platforms, different payment platforms may be used.

Can I use your product without making any in-app purchases?
Fall Guys does not require players to purchase any content to play. All purchases are optional and only provide cosmetic benefits.
